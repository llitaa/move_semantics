About Us

We are a team of engineers who fulfilled our dream of becoming a part of Western European society and currently live in Germany.

Having a look back, we would say that relocation was not a piece of cake. We want to make it different for you! That is why Move Semantics Agency was founded. Our mission is to make your relocation smooth, fast and cost-saving.

With us you get:

  • Job offers from well rated companies
  • Visa support for you, your family and pets
  • Support with your next steps abroad

How It Works

1

Send CV

When you made decision to relocate the next step is job search. This is how you benefit from our network of well rated companies. Send us your CV and we will find best suitable for you company.

  • Make sure your documents are
    up-to-date
  • Send us CV and certificates
  • Expect job offers
2

Receive Job Offer

After receiving your CV some companies will express interest in your candidacy. This is when job interviews phase begin. We support you on interviews preparation to increase your chances for success.

  • Get interview appointment
  • Prepare with our support
  • Receive job offer
3

Prepare for new Start

Final step is relocation itself. We help you resolve organizational and social questions like visa, upon-arrival documents, advise on accommodation and assimilation as well as the other steps abroad.

  • Get visa for you and your family
  • Find accomodation &
    choose best local services
  • Integrate into new society
